Artists' Need to Know

5 steps to know the process:

  1. Submit your painting in the mail below and we'll evaluate it according to our Submission Criteria.
  2. If accepted, we invite you to bring us the painting so we can safely store it and add it to our online-catalogue.
  3. The catalogue can only be accessed by café-managers to order paintings to their café for free. Café-customers can make you an offer to buy your painting.
  4. You can accept or reject the offer. By accepting the offer, you will receive 80% of the total amount, 10% goes to the café for providing its infrastructure and 10% goes to us for logistics.
  5. After successfull transaction, we will inform the café about the new owner, who can pick up the painting at the café by presenting his/her ID for confirmation.

2 steps to participate:

  1. Check out our Submission Criteria
  2. If the criteria apply, attach a picture of your painting to this Mail. Please note that by submitting your art you accept our Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy.
