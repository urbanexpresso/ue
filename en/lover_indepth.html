Art-Lovers' Knowledge

3 steps to know the concept:

  1. You've discovered a painting at a café and you really like it? Make the artist an offer he/she can't refuse!
  2. If the offer gets accepted, 80% of the total amount goes to the artist, while 10% goes to the café for its infrastructure and 10% goes to us for logistics.
  3. If the transaction is successfull, we'll let you know as soon as the painting is ready to be picked up. Just present your ID to an employee of the café and take your new painting home with you.
