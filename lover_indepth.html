Art-Lovers' Knowledge

3 steps to know the concept:

  1. At your next café-visit you might discover beautiful amateur art. Every painting has been quality-approved in two steps:
    (1) Basic Submission Criteria
    (2) Independent Expert Evaluation.
  2. You can make the artist an offer to buy the painting. The artist can either accept or deny it. If the offer gets accepted, 80% of the total amount goes to the artist, while 10% goes to the café for its infrastructure and 10% goes to us for logistics.
  3. If the transaction is successfull, we'll let you know as soon as the painting is ready to be picked up. Just present your ID to an employee of the café and take your new painting home with you.
